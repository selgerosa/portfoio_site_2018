trauma center

i worked with dani dinstman to create a gallery installation about processing trauma through confrontation and black comedy. ps: an article was written about us in squinch magazine. you can read it @squinchmag.

2017

#poster, #sticker, #zine, #installation

we used the imagery of a pediatrician's office and serene videos to mimic the prevailing idea that if you just ignore trauma it will go away. this included a wall with white vinyl text and projected nature landscapes, demotivaitonal posters, and a painting of a fairytale castle. we also made a children's toy area including handmade wooded bead and wire sculptures.

in the center of the installation, behind a bed sheet is the 'trauma center.' on one wall, there is a painting representing the internal expression of traumatic emotion and on the other a transcript of a letter from a university rejecting a sexual assault appeal with names and dates blacked out to represent how common this experience is.

for takeaways from the show, i made made stickers inspired by lisa frank and the stickers you get after getting a shot; as well as a zine with a basic breakdown of dialectical behavioral therapy.

written, designed, and coded by selina gerosa ;)
