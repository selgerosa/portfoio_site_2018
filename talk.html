talk lk u mean it

talk lk u mean it is my senior thesis. i've always been interested in the way people communicate with each other and since communication is so increasinly on the internet, i decided to focus on that. so after spending a month reading research papers, i wrote my thesis about the language of the web aka webspeak and why it matters. the book traverses the history of webspeak, starting at 1337 and working up to current day, breaks down the iterative and memetic nature of language on the web, and discusses prescriptive vs descriptive linguistic approaches. ps: i did the hardcover case-in binding for this book

2018

#sticker, #website, #installation, #book

my thesis also entailed an installation inspired by academic thesis presentations. each image panel on the back wall represents an era of meme culture with progressively compressed image quality. i also made a ton of stickers and business cards for the thesis show openning.

i intentionally made my thesis a book because i find that removing content from it's original context can introduce new perspectives. however, i also believe this research should be freely available, so i also wrote and designed a website that you can see @http://talklku​meanit.com
