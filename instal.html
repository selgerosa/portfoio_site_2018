    • forget-her-not

    collaboration with olivia gelard. our gallery was inspired by the essay grandmother spider by rebecca solnit. our show pays homage to forgotten women and our own matrilineal line. we also distributed 4 single page zines.

    2017

    at least my cactus is alive

    installation for junior typography. our prompt was a six word autobiography. this installation is about my anxiety and depression and how i process trauma in the hopes that a viewer could empathize and examine their own traumas. fun fact: i cried when i tried to explain the concept for this project to my professor (and half of my class).

    2016

    social media quiz

    this installation was made for junior graphic design. all of the text is vinyl and the rest is gaffer tape. my concept was a teen-magazine style quiz playing on the complex unspoken culture of social media. fun fact: i love social media !! don't believe me? check this out.
