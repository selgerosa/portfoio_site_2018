all about me

my name is selina gerosa. i’m a designer currently living in new york. i’m a recent grad from boston university; got my bfa in graphic design (and minor in computer science). i’m interested in internet culture, true crime documentaries, and niche cartoons. in my spare time i practice bookbinding and redesign my website.

i enjoy creating websites (html/css/js), logos and branding, posters, video production (after effects, premier pro), publication and bookmaking, ad design, illustration, social media management, and working with the adobe cloud suite.

about the site:

this site is based on late 1990s/early 2000s era web design (think myspace and geocities) and contemporary brutalist web design. i have always been fascinated by internet culture and aesthetics, especially those that highlight the unique voice of their creator. this site combines the unbridled chaos of the early web with the aggressive and minimalist aspects of brutalism.

ps: you should click on the little pics that are floating around the site; they go to various web experiments and mini-websites i've designed. however, please be aware that some of them feature flashing animation. alternatively, you can see all the sites @https://selinas-site-experiments.netlify.com
