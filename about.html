    about the site:

    this site is based on late 1990s/early 2000s era web design (think myspace and geocities) and contemporary brutalist web design. i have always been fascinated with internet culture and aesthetics, especially those that highlight the unique voice of their creator. this site combines the unbridled chaos of the early web with the aggressive and minimalist aspects of brutalism.

    ps: you should click on the little pics that are floating around the site; they go to various web experiments and mini-websites i've designed. however, please be aware that some of them feature flashing animation.
