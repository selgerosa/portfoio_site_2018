forget-her-not

olivia gelard and i collaborated on a gallery show inspired by the essay grandmother spider by rebecca solnit. our show pays homage to forgotten women and our own matrilineal line. we embraced the feminine form of art ('craft') by making yarn webs, charcoal portraits, and miniposters with rhinestones, glitter, and pink paper. we also distributed 4 single page zines.

2017
